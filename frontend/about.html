About NexusMeet

NexusMeet is a production-grade WebRTC browser meeting platform designed for maximum accessibility and security. Built from the ground up to provide seamless video conferencing without the overhead of user accounts, passwords, or complex software installations.

Zero-Login Architecture

We believe you shouldn't have to surrender personal data to join a quick meeting. NexusMeet utilizes dynamic routing and WebRTC Mesh networking to establish secure, peer-to-peer connections instantly. Just share a link, enter your name, and you're in.

Privacy First Recording

Unlike traditional platforms that store recordings in the cloud, NexusMeet's selective recording feature (via the MediaRecorder API) saves video files strictly locally to the host's device. We never see, touch, or store your video feeds.

Technical Foundation

  • Frontend: Vanilla Javascript, HTML5, Tailwind CSS.
  • Signaling: Node.js, Express, and Socket.io.
  • Streaming: Native browser WebRTC API.
